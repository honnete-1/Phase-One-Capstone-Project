Behind the App

Built by Honnete for anyone who has ever forgotten a book they meant to read.

Why I built this

Most people have a mental list of books they want to read, and most people lose that list. Book Explorer exists to fix that. Search for something that interests you, save what catches your eye as a favorite book, and come back to it whenever you're ready. No account needed. No emails. Just you and your next great read.

What went into building it

  • HTML Building pages that are structured, readable, and accessible to everyone
  • Tailwind Designing a clean, responsive layout that works on any screen size
  • JavaScript Making the app interactive — clicks, updates, real-time feedback
  • Async JS Connecting to the Open Library API to pull in real book data
  • localStorage Keeping your saved books safe even after you close the tab
  • GitHub Managing the project with branches, commits, and version control
